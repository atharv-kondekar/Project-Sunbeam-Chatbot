CATEGORY: Internship & Training

OVERVIEW:
Sunbeam provides hands-on industrial training internships focused on practical skills, project development, and mentorship. Durations: 1 Month, 2 Months, 6 Months.

STRUCTURE BY DURATION:

1 MONTH:
- Comprehensive training + mini project
- Mode: Offline at campus

2 MONTHS:
- Technology training sessions + 2 mini projects
- 1 Offline + 1 Online

6 MONTHS:
- Full training + Mini Project + Major Project
- 1 Offline + 1 Online (Enhanced features project)

BENEFITS:
- Practical industry skills
- Real-world project experience
- Mentor guidance
- Evaluation & feedback reviews
- Certificate on completion
- Placement preparation support

AVAILABLE DOMAINS:
- MERN
- Android
- Internet of Things
- ARM Microcontroller
- Generative AI
- Java Programming
- .NET
- Web Programming using Python
- Data Science (ML)
- Cyber Security
